Namo Express-company overview

Namo Expo brand in South India which caters to the International Courier Express and logistics needs of all major corporate companies and multi-nationals. We offer both tailor made and standardized integrated logistics & International courier solutions ranging from supply chain management , warehousing and value added services like single pickup and multi delivery points. We specialize in delivery through courier partners, warehousing and other related services. Over the years, we have expanded our operations and moved into such specialized areas as, International Express, Express parcel, Packers & Movers, Express Logistics, Transportation, Warehousing, Customs Clearance and Cold Chain Services.
